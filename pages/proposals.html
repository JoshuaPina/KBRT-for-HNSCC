Documentation Hub

This page contains all proposals, posts, and publications related to the Project Asclepius initiative. Each entry provides a brief overview of the content, with options to read more.

The proposals and publications listed here are part of our ongoing efforts to advance research in the field of head and neck squamous cell carcinoma (HNSCC) using machine learning and mathematical modeling. We aim to optimize treatment planning and improve patient outcomes through innovative approaches.
