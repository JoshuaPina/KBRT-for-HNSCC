Meet The Team

We are a team of dedicated technical researchers at Georgia State University, working on an interdisciplinary mission to help improve cancer treatment. Our goal is to leverage our technical and pragmatic skills in machine learning and mathematical modeling to improve treatment planning for head and neck squamous cell carcinoma (HNSCC).
